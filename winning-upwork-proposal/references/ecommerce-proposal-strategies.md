# E-commerce & Shopify/WordPress Proposal Strategies

## Common E-commerce Job Categories

### 1. Store Setup & Configuration
**Opening Options**:
- "What specific features or functionalities do you want on your [product type] website (e.g., abandoned cart recovery, inventory management, multi-channel selling, or subscription options)?"
- "What's your vision for your [product type] store, and what challenges are you trying to solve with the setup?"
- "Are you looking to launch your [product type] store but concerned about [specific challenge - payment processing, inventory sync, etc.]?"
**Focus Areas**:
- Store setup and configuration
- Payment gateway integration
- Shipping configuration
- Product import/export

### 2. Conversion Optimization
**Opening Options**:
- "Are you looking to increase your online sales but concerned about [specific challenge - cart abandonment, low conversion rates, etc.]?"
- "What specific conversion challenges are you facing with your [platform] store?"
- "Are you trying to improve your conversion rates but unsure which areas to focus on first?"
**Focus Areas**:
- Checkout process optimization
- Product page improvements
- A/B testing implementation
- Trust signal integration

### 3. Performance & Speed Optimization
**Opening Options**:
- "The [performance issue] you're experiencing typically stems from [likely causes like unoptimized images,过多 plugins, etc.], which I can resolve by [general approach]."
- "Are you looking to improve your site speed but concerned about [specific challenge - Core Web Vitals scores, mobile performance, etc.]?"
- "What performance metrics are you trying to achieve with your [platform] site?"
**Focus Areas**:
- Site speed improvements
- Core Web Vitals optimization
- Image optimization
- Code minification

### 4. Custom Feature Development
**Opening Options**:
- "What specific functionality are you looking to add to your [platform] store (e.g., custom product configurators, loyalty programs, bulk ordering, or advanced reporting)?"
- "Are you looking to add custom functionality to your [platform] store but concerned about [specific challenge - integration, scalability, etc.]?"
- "What unique features would give your [platform] store a competitive advantage?"
**Focus Areas**:
- Custom app development
- Third-party integrations
- Unique business logic implementation
- Advanced functionality

### 5. Migration Projects
**Opening Options**:
- "Are you looking to migrate your store from [current platform] to [target platform] but concerned about [data loss, SEO impact, etc.]?"
- "What's your migration timeline for moving from [current platform] to [target platform]?"
- "Are you planning to migrate your [platform] store but worried about [specific concern - downtime, data integrity, etc.]?"
**Focus Areas**:
- Data migration
- SEO preservation
- URL structure mapping
- Third-party service integration
