# English Text Improver

This skill enhances English text to have a professional yet soft tone while maintaining a conversational, natural style. The skill analyzes the context of the provided text and improves it to sound approachable, polished, and human-like without appearing robotic or AI-generated.

## Purpose

Effective communication requires text that sounds natural, approachable, and professionally polished. This skill transforms text to achieve a balance between professionalism and warmth, ensuring the improved text sounds like it was written by a thoughtful human rather than generated by AI.

## Before Implementation

Gather context to ensure successful implementation:

| Source | Gather |
|--------|--------|
| **Text to Improve** | Original text that needs tone enhancement |
| **Context** | Purpose, audience, and intended use of the text |
| **Tone Requirements** | Desired level of formality, warmth, and approachability |
| **Skill References** | Best practices from `references/tone-guidelines.md` and conversational patterns from `references/conversational-writing.md` |

## Key Improvement Principles

### 1. Context Analysis
- Understand the purpose and audience of the text
- Identify the appropriate level of formality
- Recognize the emotional undertones needed
- Consider the relationship between writer and reader

### 2. Professional Softness
- Use approachable language without sacrificing professionalism
- Balance warmth with credibility
- Maintain respect while being conversational
- Build trust through natural-sounding communication

### 3. Conversational Quality
- Use natural sentence rhythms and structures
- Incorporate contractions where appropriate
- Vary sentence lengths for readability
- Include transitional phrases that connect ideas smoothly

### 4. Natural Flow
- Avoid repetitive or formulaic language
- Use varied vocabulary without being overly complex
- Maintain logical progression of ideas
- Ensure smooth transitions between concepts

## Improvement Process

### Step 1: Analyze the Original Text
- Identify the core message and intent
- Assess the current tone and style
- Determine the target audience
- Recognize any specific requirements or constraints

### Step 2: Apply Tone Adjustments
- Replace formal/robotic language with natural alternatives
- Add warmth and personality while maintaining professionalism
- Ensure the text feels like it comes from a real person
- Balance approachability with authority

### Step 3: Enhance Conversational Elements
- Use active voice where appropriate
- Include natural connectors and transitions
- Add subtle personal touches without overdoing it
- Ensure the rhythm flows naturally when read aloud

### Step 4: Polish and Refine
- Check for consistency in tone throughout
- Verify that the message remains clear and effective
- Ensure the improved text aligns with the original intent
- Make final adjustments for maximum impact

## Common Transformations

### From Robotic to Natural
- "The aforementioned solution provides optimal results" → "This approach works really well"
- "It is important to note that" → "Keep in mind that" or "Notice that"
- "Subsequent to the implementation" → "After we put this in place"

### From Cold to Warm
- "Your request has been processed" → "I've taken care of your request"
- "We regret to inform you" → "I wanted to reach out about" or "I need to share"
- "Per your inquiry" → "As you asked about"

### From Complex to Clear
- "Utilization of the system" → "Using the system"
- "Facilitate the process" → "Help with the process" or "Make the process easier"
- "At this point in time" → "Now" or "Right now"

## Guidelines for Natural-Sounding Text

### Do
- Use contractions naturally (it's, we're, don't, won't)
- Include personal pronouns (we, you, I) when appropriate
- Vary sentence structure and length
- Use active voice predominantly
- Include natural transitions and connections
- Speak directly to the reader when appropriate
- Add personality while maintaining professionalism

### Don't
- Overuse formal or academic language
- Write in a stiff, robotic manner
- Use unnecessarily complex words when simpler ones work
- Sound like an instruction manual
- Ignore the emotional context of the message
- Make everything sound identical regardless of purpose
- Use AI-generated sounding phrases

## Validation Checklist

Before finalizing the improved text, ensure:
- [ ] Tone matches the intended audience and purpose
- [ ] Text sounds like it was written by a person, not an AI
- [ ] Professionalism is maintained while adding warmth
- [ ] Message clarity is preserved or enhanced
- [ ] Language feels natural and conversational
- [ ] The original intent and meaning remain intact
- [ ] Text flows well when read aloud
- [ ] No robotic or formulaic phrases remain
- [ ] Appropriate level of formality for the context
- [ ] Personal connection is established without being overly casual
